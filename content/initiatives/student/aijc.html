AIJC

Artificial Intelligence Journal Club

Description

AI Journal Club is a graduate student reading group that meets weekly to talk about new and classic papers in the field of artificial intelligence. Each week, we have someone present a paper they found interesting, accompanied by an informal discussion. Past topics have included reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, bias and accountability in AI, natural language processing, and analogical reasoning.

Our goal is to help students stay up-to-date in the fast-changing field of AI and provide a place for students from different labs and programs to share their research.

Join our Slack channel at aijournalclub.slack.com for meeting times, AI discussion, and more.

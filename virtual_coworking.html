Virtual Coworking

Bringing focus back during challenging times

Description

Having trouble focusing on your work while working from home?
So are we, this is why we created the CS Virtual Coworking space!

Join us in the coworking room to work on your own tasks.
Come see, be seen, and join other students who are also focusing on their own tasks.

Students will be there every day from 3PM to 5PM, and you are welcome to stay past 5PM.

A new dedicated slack channel called #virtual-coworking on the CS++ Slack Workspace is also available.
If you're not on that slack workspace yet, don't miss out, head here.

Information

Hosted sessions:

  • Monday to Friday
  • 3PM to 5PM
  • From May 25th to June 5th

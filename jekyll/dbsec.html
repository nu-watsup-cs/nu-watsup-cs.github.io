DB-Sec

Database, security, and privacy Reading Group

Description

While data and analytics play a large role in organizing and influencing our daily lives, the privacy and security implications are often ignored. Our reading group is focused on exploring the intersection of databases, privacy, and security.

By reading papers from all three areas, we can see the current state of the art, as well as the future systems that we need to build.
